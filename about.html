About ACI

ACI (Advanced Cotton Industries) has been at the forefront of textile import, storage, and distribution since its founding. Our goal is to offer high-quality fabrics at competitive prices while maintaining ethical sourcing and sustainable warehousing.

Based in Greece, ACI manages over 5,000 square meters of textile storage and partners with reliable international suppliers from Europe, Asia, and the Americas.

We take pride in our experienced team and longstanding customer relationships that span decades. Quality, consistency, and trust are our core values.
